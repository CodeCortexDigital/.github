## 🏢 Who We Are

**Code Cortex** is a Pakistan-based **AI-first software engineering company** delivering intelligent digital solutions for startups, enterprises, educational institutions, and public-sector organizations.

We combine **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Enterprise Software**, and **Modern Web Technologies** to build scalable products that solve real business problems.

Our engineering philosophy is simple:

> **Every product we build has intelligence at its core.**

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

**🚩 Our Mission**
To help organizations move faster and smarter by embedding artificial intelligence into the software that runs their business — not as an add-on, but as a foundation.

**🔭 Our Vision**
To be recognized globally as an AI-first engineering partner that turns ambitious ideas into production-grade, intelligent systems used by real organizations at scale.
